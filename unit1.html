    The Online World

    This unit provides an introduction to the modern online world. It is helping me to expand my knowledge of online services and investigate the technology and software that support them. I have learnt about a range of services including emails, online data storage, collaborative software, search engines and blogging. This will result in an internet of services, objects and infrastructure (ubiquitous computing) which will radically change our lives. We will exploring the common hardware devices that make up technology systems, including touch screens, printers and components such as the processor, buses and memory which are the internal building blocks of a computer.
