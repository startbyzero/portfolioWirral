    Networking

    This Unit is very interesting in fact I didn't think I could fall in love with this one. I am learning how to create a simple LAN line until I get to create more complicated structures. I created some ethernet cables that require a crimper to cut the cables enough to see the wires, then we tested everything with testers. Another very interesting topic to cover are cables with different uses, UTP, Optic Cable and so on. I am fascinated by this unit because it makes you understand how complicated the internet world is and above all its dangers regarding Web security, another crucial topic of networking.
